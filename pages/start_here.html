Welcome to Realistic Stock Trader

For new users, welcome! This guide will help you get started on your stock-trading journey!

Step 1. Create an Account

Sign up using the Sign Up page. After creating an account you may log in and gain access to buring and selling stocks, as well as developing your stock portfolio!

Step 2. The different pages and keywords!

  • Stocks - View the different stocks available for buying and selling, including current prices and recent changes.
  • Portfolio - View your current estimates value in stock holdings and monitor the gains or losses!
  • Profile - View the details of your account including First and Last name, email, password, and current balance! Your profile can be edited/deleted from this page.
  • Order History - View your transactions and details about each transaction.

Step 3. Adding funds

After account creation, you can add funds from your dashboard by clicking the Add funds button.

BE CAREFUL stock prices are volatile and can change rapidly, so please invest wisely!

Step 4. Buying and selling

  • Open the Buy page and select a stock. Enter quantity of how many shares and confirm the purchase!

    • Note, the price listed next to the stock is the price per 1 share!

  • For selling shares, go to Sell and choose how many shares of a stock to sell!
  • All orders will update your account balance and transaction history immediately!

Step 5. Tracking stock/portfolio value

Individual stock value will update to display how much value and % change has occurred since the last price per share update!

Your portfolio value will also display the % change of all of your stock value per price update!

Step 6. Support and Feedback

If you find any issues, questions, concerns, or want to request a feature, use the Support page!

Administrators will be able to view the submitted dashboard and will assist you as soon as possible!

Step 7. Market Hours and Holidays

Normal market hours are shown on the About Us page.

Trades are only allowed while the market is open.

The page will list the Holidays and the market will be closed during those days.

Tips for New Users

  • Start small! If you are new, try to exchange a few shares at a time to understand how the process works!
  • Keep an eye on the percent change and difference columns to learn how volatility affects your holdings!
  • Use the transaction history to view your past activity to see which stocks are performing well and which stocks are not!
